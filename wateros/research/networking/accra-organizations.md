Major International and National Funders

World Bank Ghana – Funds large-scale water and sanitation infrastructure projects through the Ghana Water and Sanitation Program.

African Development Bank (AfDB) – Supports urban water supply and sanitation projects across Africa, including Ghana.

UNICEF Ghana – Focuses on WASH initiatives in schools and communities through its WASH Section.

USAID Ghana – Funds a variety of development programs, including the WASH (Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene) Program.

WaterAid Ghana – An international NGO dedicated to providing access to safe water, sanitation, and hygiene.

Millennium Development Authority (MiDA) – Implements programs funded by the Millennium Challenge Corporation, often including water projects.

Safe Water Network (operating H2OME! brand) – Works to expand access to safe water through small-scale systems and social enterprises.

European Union Delegation to Ghana – Provides funding and technical assistance to the water and sanitation sector through the Water Sector Support Program.

Ghana Water Company Limited (GWCL) – Engages in partnership programs with donors and private organizations to improve water delivery infrastructure.

The Conrad N. Hilton Foundation – A major global funder of water projects, with targeted investments in Ghana.

The Coca-Cola Foundation (Replenish Africa Initiative - RAIN) – Funds water access and replenishment projects across Ghana.

Global Affairs Canada – Supports water, sanitation, and hygiene initiatives through bilateral and NGO partnerships.

DANIDA (Danish International Development Agency) – Long-standing supporter of Ghana’s water and sanitation sector.

Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation – Provides water and sanitation funding in Ghana through implementing partners.
